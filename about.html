My Journey

My fascination with high-performance systems started early. Over the past decade, I've seen Redis evolve from a simple key-value store into a complex, multi-model database engine. I've spent thousands of hours debugging race conditions, optimizing memory usage, and architecting clusters for global scale.

I founded Velocis to bridge the gap between "it works" and "it's optimized." Many teams use Redis, but few use it to its full potential. My mission is to change that.

Our History

Velocis was born out of a simple observation: most engineering teams treat Redis as a black box. In 2020, after years of fixing the same performance bottlenecks at major tech companies, I decided to build a consultancy dedicated to one thing—making Redis faster.

What started as a one-person mission has grown into a global network of performance specialists. We've helped over 200 companies reduce latency and scale their data infrastructure without the overhead of enterprise-sized consultancies.
